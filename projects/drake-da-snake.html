drake da snake: a nokia inspired game

December 2024

project link

A cheeky reimagining of the classic Nokia Snake game where Drake (or YOU) must desperately slither to collect clout while being chased by Kendrick. Each victory unlocks unique Drake memes. Available on desktop use only — coming to mobile soon!

This project was fun to work on! It stemmed from several inspirations:

  • nostalgia for classic Nokia Snake gameplay (although I didn’t achieve this fully, given feedback and reflection)
  • interest in creating engaging experiences
  • team kendrick; interested in roasting drake post lawsuit debacle
  • incorporate meme culture into gameplay mechanics (let’s take playful to another level!)
